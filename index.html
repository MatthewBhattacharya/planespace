Planespace — Rules

Goal

Place polygons on the grid to score the most points. Score depends on the area of your shape AND its geometric properties.

On Your Turn

  • Click 3–8 grid dots to select vertices
  • Click Place Shape to lock it in
  • Shapes may share edges/vertices but cannot overlap interiors
  • If you cannot place, click Pass
  • Two consecutive passes end the game

Scoring — pure lookup, no math beyond addition

Total = Base Score (by area) + Shape Bonus (by type & size bracket) + Gem Bonus. No square roots, no multiplying — just find your numbers on the tables below and add them up.

Area cap: No single shape may have area > 9 grid-unit² (prevents one-move board domination).

Gem Bonus: +10 per ★ Gem vertex used — but each Gem pays out only once, to whichever shape touches it first. Copying your opponent's gem-touching move earns nothing.

Blocking: you may share an edge or vertex freely with your own shapes, but you may only touch an opponent's shape at a single vertex — sharing a full edge with their shape is not allowed.

Geometry You'll Learn

  • Area: Shoelace formula — base score = √area × 28 (diminishing returns)
  • Right triangle: a²+b²=c² — check with Pythagorean theorem
  • 45-45-90: isosceles right triangle — the two legs are equal
  • Quad hierarchy: Square ⊂ Rect ∩ Rhombus ⊂ Parallelogram ⊂ Trapezoid
  • Collinearity: 3 gems lie on one line — can you find which ones?
  • Convexity: all cross products of edges share the same sign
